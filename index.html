For Parents

Honest answers
to real questions.

Is placement 100% guaranteed? +
No university can legally guarantee 100% placement. At JECRC, 85–90% of eligible students are placed, with the top 30% averaging 10.7 LPA. Mandatory 6-month internships significantly boost employability.
Is JEE mandatory for B.Tech? +
No. Admission is merit-based (Class 12 marks) or entrance-based (JEE Main). A good JEE percentile qualifies you for a higher scholarship bracket.
How safe is the campus for girls? +
JECRC has separate boys and girls hostels with 2,000+ capacity, 24/7 CCTV security, a dedicated medical centre, and contactless food delivery. Located in Sitapura Industrial Area, Jaipur — 25–30 min from city centre.
Are there any hidden charges? +
None. JECRC publishes a full fee schedule including hostel (₹90K–₹1.5L/yr), bus (₹50K/yr), and application fee (₹1,000). B.Tech total fee is ₹3.64L–₹11.1L for 4 years depending on specialization.
Can my child transfer to JECRC in 2nd year? +
Yes. JECRC accepts lateral entry for diploma holders (direct 2nd year B.Tech) and migration/transfer from other universities, subject to seat availability.
What popular specializations are trending? +
B.Tech CSE — AI & ML (with IBM / Xebia), B.Tech Cyber Security (EC-Council USA certified), B.Tech Cloud Computing (Microsoft / AWS), and MBA FinTech are the fastest-filling programs. Average packages: 8–16 LPA.
